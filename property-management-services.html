Property Management Services

Our Services

ER Property Solution specializes in managing and selling single-family homes, condominiums, duplexes, multi-family properties, small & large apartment complexes, commercial centers and everything in between all across Southern California. Our expert property management team takes on the day-to-day chores of managing your rental property so you can enjoy and protect your successful investment worry free.

Tenant Finding

ER Property Solutions have become experts in finding high quality tenants for our client’s rental properties. We go through a very in-depth screening process including finding the tenant’s official credit score, criminal history, court cases, bankruptcies, evictions, employment verification, income verification, past residence verification, and more. We are proud to say we’ve never had to evict a tenant that we have found for our owners.

Marketing

Because of the technology expertise among the ER Property Solutions team, we have mastered the online marketing space and ensure we fill your vacancy as soon as possible. We typically never take more than 3 weeks to fill your property with high quality tenants because we have been able to market it so effectively across all rental avenues which heavily increases demand and inquiries.

Maintenance

Since we have been doing this for many years, we have been through hundreds of service professionals and now have an amazing team for any maintenance at your property whatsoever. Everything from inexpensive handymen who do great work to full blown general contractors and everything in between. We also do not charge any fee on maintenance or repairs whatsoever like other companies do. We don’t think we should be incentivized to find repairs at your property!

Accounting

We have strong technology, real estate, and property management experience among the team. We have our entire business running on an efficient, optimized software system that gives you access to your owner portal which consists of a full accounting ledger of all payments from tenants, repair costs, fees, rent sent to you electronically, and more. You can easy pull your accounting ledger or run real time property accounting reports for your CPA.

Rent Collection

With technology being as advanced as it is, ER Property Solutions makes rent payment one of the easiest aspects of being a renter. They don’t even have to leave the house. Renters can pay online through an ACH transfer which is connected by our secure software. If cash is primarily used in the household, renters can visit their nearest 7/11 or convenient store which supports the PayNearMe software and pay cash there. ER Property Solutions makes it easier than ever to pay rent so there are no more reasons for tenants to give excuses about unpaid rent!

Inspections

ER Property Solutions do inspections of your property for many different reasons at no additional cost. Inspections are an essential aspect of property management as they allow us to give updated reports with the condition of your investment property. ER Property Solutions drives by your property once every 2-3 weeks in order to ensure it’s been taking care of and if there is anything we need to enforce with the tenants.

Legality & Contracts

As an owner of an investment property, the one place that no investor likes to be in is court. For that reason, ER Property Solutions works with some of the best legal teams that specialize in tenant/landlord law to ensure that each action protects our clients. With the ever-changing laws in Southern California, it is extremely important to have a strong, experienced legal team on your side to mitigate any risk of litigation and liability with your tenants and properties.

Our contracts are individually prepared in order to establish a great foundation to which both parties feel comfortable signing and moving forward with. Having a detailed and well written contract allows both parties to clearly understand expectations and compensations. Apart from creating contracts, our legal teams also prepare tenant notices to ensure that the latest and most updated lease agreements, disclosures, and city ordinances are in place when sent out and signed.
