Real Estate Agent Services

As experienced, licensed real estate agents, we have helped thousands of sellers & buyers with selling and purchasing properties.

Leasing

Any Casa has become experts in finding high quality tenants for our client’s rental properties. We go through a very in-depth screening process which includes pulling up the tenant’s official credit score, criminal history, court cases, bankruptcies, evictions, employment verification, income verification, past residence verification, and more. We are proud to say we’ve never had to evict a tenant that we have found for our clients.

Marketing

Because of the technology expertise among the Any Casa team, we have mastered the online marketing space and ensure we fill your vacancy as soon as possible. We typically never take more than 3 weeks to fill your property with high quality tenants because we have been able to market it so effectively across all rental avenues which heavily increases demand and inquiries.

Maintenance

We have been through hundreds of vendors over the years and now have an amazing team for any and all maintenance requests at your property. Everything from inexpensive handymen who do great work to full blown general contractors and everything in between. You can feel relieved at all times knowing that your property is being maintained by experienced vendors.

Accounting

We have strong technology, real estate, and property management experience among the team. We have our entire business running on an efficient, optimized software system that gives you access to your owner portal which consists of a full accounting ledger of all payments from tenants, repair costs, fees, rent sent to you electronically, and more. You can easy pull your accounting ledger or run real time property accounting reports for your CPA.

Rent Collection

With real estate & technology expertise, Any Casa has implemented effective software platforms to ensure rent is collected by any payment type necessary and tracked perfectly.

Annual Inspections

Any Casa performs annual inspections during lease renewal periods to ensure your property is being cared for.

Legal & Contracts

Any Casa has legal services on a subscription based model so every team member has direct access to legal assistance to ensure every decision with properties, tenants, vendors, and owners is done properly.
